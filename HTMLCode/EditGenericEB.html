Project Delivery Delivering Value for Money
Topic: 
Value Management
Category: 
Delivering value for money through design
 
Headline:
Kier supported NHS South Gloucestershire PCT to achieve scheme affordability during the development of Yate Community Health and Children's Centre.
Description (who, what, when):
Our Design Manager facilitated an early Value Management workshop with key stakeholders, to prioritise their operational needs. Using the Paired Objectives Technique to compare user objectives, we identified that separate receptions were a low priority for service managers. Our new design solution improved space efficiency by establishing a shared waiting area, rather than 3 separate areas. We also benchmarked room utilisation against historic patient throughput, leading to the development of flexible use treatment/consulting rooms, to increase space utilisation.
Benefits (outcomes):
Without compromising clinical compliance or functionality we reduced building area from 3600m2 to 2700m2, and saved the client £2.2m, ensuring business case approval first time by the SHA.
